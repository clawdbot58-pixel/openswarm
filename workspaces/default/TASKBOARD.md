```markdown

- **Status:** pending | in_progress | done | blocked
- **Priority:** high | medium | low
- **Created by:** <agent>
- **Assigned to:** <agent>
- **Parent workflow:** <workflow-id>
- **Created:** <timestamp>
- **Updated:** <timestamp>
- **Description:** <what needs to be done>
- **Notes:** <comments, updates>
```
